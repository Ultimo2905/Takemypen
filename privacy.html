Privacy Policy

07.22.2019


Whenever the client accesses our website, they must be aware that such information as: IP address, browser type, and access time are collected automatically and stored but only for the internal company’s use with the aim to improve the service we provide and keep making it more efficient and technically sufficient. The information collected also helps us improve the quality of the service given by the Support Team, it is also used for assembling statistics of website utilization. The personal information is collected only if such information is required for authentication, or a visitor themselves reaches the Support Team via phone, chat, message, or email, and provides us with personal information on their behalf.

Regardless of the fact that the personal information imposed (such as names, email and billing address, phone numbers etc.) is stored in our database, it is protected and securely kept in our database and is exposed only for the company’s internal use. You are encouraged to provide us only with the valid information that might be used to check your payment or identity. The legitimacy of information guarantees you the quality service that you are to receive.

Your email address will be used only for our collaborative purposes: sending updates regarding changes of our policies, Terms and Conditions, or contacting you in regards to the product ordered. Your email address can also be used for sending necessary documents for the order completion, or receiving the completed work.

The email address and phone number provided, gives us the right to assume that you have voluntarily agreed to be contacted by our representatives in the matter of your order, clarification of its details, verification of your payments, and promotional purposes.

If you refuse to receive promotional emails or calls with various offers and discounts that we offer to our loyal customers, you may request unsubscribing by sending us an email with a corresponding request.

Be aware that in case our company needs to verify your payment or other details, additional information may be solicited beside the information that was already provided when registering on our website. The neglectance of such request may prevent your order from its successful and on-time delivery.

Our company reassures you that all the billing information given (billing details, credit card information) to proceed with your payment for the product is collected only by the secure payment processor and is not accessed by our company.

We guarantee that none of your personal information is revealed or shared with any third parties without your written approval. The only information that you agree to have shared is the information provided in the order details (instructions, files etc.) that serves for successful and high-quality assignment completion. However, such information is allocated only to an executive of your assignment.

You can easily edit, change or delete your phone number and name in your profile, however your email address and the order instructions can be altered only with the help of the Support Team who you can reach 24/7. After receiving an inquiry about removing your account from our database you can expect it done within 14 days.
